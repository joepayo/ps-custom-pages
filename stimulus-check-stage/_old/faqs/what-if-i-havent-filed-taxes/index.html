In order to receive your stimulus check, you now have to file your taxes. For the first two payments, those who were not required to file taxes because of low earnings could enter their information through a portal on the IRS website. That portal is now closed. If you did not file taxes in 2018 or 2019, or if you did not use the IRS payment portal for the first two checks, you need to file taxes in 2020 in order to receive your stimulus check. You can get started using the IRS Free File program.
