We’ve heard reports that some individuals did not receive the dependent payments they are eligible for. If you receive Social Security retirement benefits or SSI, and have dependents, it’s possible the IRS sent out your payment without accounting for your dependents. For others, it’s unclear why they didn’t receive the full amount they are eligible for.

Regardless, if you did not receive the full amount you believe you were eligible for, you can claim the Recovery Rebate Credit to receive a refund or credit on your 2020 taxes, equal to the value of the missed stimulus payments.
