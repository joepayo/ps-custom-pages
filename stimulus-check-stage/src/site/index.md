intro_title: Who is eligible for a Stimulus Check?
intro_text: The vast majority of Michigan residents are eligible to receive a stimulus check from the federal government this year. Even if you have no income, you are still eligible, but need to take action to receive your stimulus payment. <strong>This includes individuals with low or no earnings who normally don't file taxes.</strong> Every American adult earning less than $75,000 (or couples earning less than $150,000) is eligible for a stimulus check from the federal government this year. This includes individuals with low or no earnings, who normally don't file taxes.
