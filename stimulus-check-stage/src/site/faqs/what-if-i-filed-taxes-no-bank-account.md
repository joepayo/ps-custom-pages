question: What if I filed taxes in 2018 or 2019, but the IRS doesn’t have my bank account information?
id: faq-4
date: 2020-01-04
---
**If you don’t think the IRS has your bank account information on file,** enter your account information using the <a href='https://www.irs.gov/coronavirus/economic-impact-payments' rel='noopener noreferrer' target='_blank'>IRS website</a>. This will enable the IRS to deposit your check directly.
<br><br>
**If you don’t have a bank account,** you can create a safe and affordable one at a <a href='https://joinbankon.org/home/remotebanking/' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>Bank On certified institution</a>. If you don’t want to sign up for a bank account you can link your prepaid debit card or <a href='https://cash.app/help/us/en-us/1018-cash-app-the-stimulus-and-you' target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>Cash App</a> instead.
