question: How do I get the stimulus payment?
id: faq-7
date: 2020-01-07
---
**The primary way the federal government will send stimulus payments** is by depositing money directly into people’s bank accounts based on tax records.
<br><br>
This means that many Americans will not need to do anything to get their stimulus payment. The money will be deposited into your bank account and you’ll get a letter from the IRS confirming the payment.
