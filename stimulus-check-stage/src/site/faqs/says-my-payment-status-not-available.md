question: I went on the <a href="https://www.irs.gov/coronavirus/get-my-payment" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">IRS Get My Payment</a> site and it says that my payment status is not available. What do I do?
id: faq-17
date: 2020-01-17
---
Your information may not appear on the <a href='https://www.irs.gov/coronavirus/get-my-payment' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>Get My Payment</a> site if you didn't file your taxes in 2018 or 2019, if you recently filed taxes and they haven't been processed yet, if you recently entered payment information on the <a href='https://www.irs.gov/coronavirus/non-filers-enter-payment-info-here' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>IRS site for non-filers</a>, or if you receive Social Security benefits. You may also get this message if you are not eligible for a payment. Here is <a href='https://www.irs.gov/coronavirus/get-my-payment-frequently-asked-questions#elig' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>a list</a> of the people who arenâ€™t eligible for stimulus payments.
<br><br>
If you haven't filed taxes, you must either file taxes, or, if you aren't required to file, enter your payment information on the IRS site for non-filers.  If you've recently filed your taxes, or entered your payment information, check back once per day on the <a href='https://www.irs.gov/coronavirus/get-my-payment' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>Get My Payment</a> site to see if it's been processed. If you receive Social Security benefits, it's likely the IRS has already sent your payment to the same account you receive your benefits.
<br><br>
For more information, visit the <a href='https://www.irs.gov/coronavirus/economic-impact-payment-information-center' target='_blank' rel='noreferrer noopener'>IRS Economic Impact Payment Information Center</a>, which is being continuously updated.
