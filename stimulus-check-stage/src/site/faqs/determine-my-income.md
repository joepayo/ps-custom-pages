tags: faqs
question: How will the IRS determine my income for the stimulus payment?
id: faq-2
date: 2020-01-02
---
**If you’ve already filed your 2019 taxes,** your 2019 gross adjusted income will be used to determine eligibility. If you haven’t filed your 2019 taxes yet, the IRS will use your 2018 gross adjusted income to determine eligibility.
