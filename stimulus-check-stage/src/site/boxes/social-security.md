You can file a federal tax return <a href="https://apps.irs.gov/app/freeFile/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">for free online</a>:
* If you receive Social Security benefits and have dependent(s), you need to report your dependents to the IRS to receive your $500 credit. You can enter information on dependents on the <a href='https://www.irs.gov/coronavirus/economic-impact-payments' target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>IRS site for non-filers</a>.
* SSI recipients who have dependent children and did not file 2018 or 2019 taxes need to **<a href='https://www.irs.gov/coronavirus/non-filers-enter-payment-info-here' target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>act by Tuesday, May 5,</a>** in order to receive additional payments for their eligible children quickly.
