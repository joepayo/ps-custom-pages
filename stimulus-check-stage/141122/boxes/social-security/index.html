You can file a federal tax return for free online:

  • If you receive Social Security benefits and have dependent(s), you need to report your dependents to the IRS to receive your $500 credit. You can enter information on dependents on the IRS site for non-filers.
  • SSI recipients who have dependent children and did not file 2018 or 2019 taxes need to act by Tuesday, May 5, in order to receive additional payments for their eligible children quickly.
