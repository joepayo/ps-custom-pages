The IRS will wire funds to bank accounts in late March if they have your bank account information. Estimates suggest paper checks may also be sent out by late March. You can track your payment through the Get My Payment tool.

If your 2020 income differs substantially from your 2019 income, and the IRS needs to send you additional funds, these will likely come later. The IRS has set a deadline of December 31, 2021, for sending out all checks.
